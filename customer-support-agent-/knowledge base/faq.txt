ABC Technologies provides answers to several frequently asked customer questions through its knowledge base. Customers wishing to upgrade or downgrade their subscription plan can do so through the Billing section of the customer portal, and any changes generally become effective during the next billing cycle. Customers who forget their password can select the "Forgot Password" option on the login page, after which password reset instructions will be sent to their registered email address.

Refund requests are evaluated according to the company's refund policy and require review by a human supervisor before approval. Customers who require assistance can contact support at any time through live chat, email, or the customer portal, as customer support services are available twenty-four hours a day.

New customers receive a fourteen-day free trial that includes access to all Professional Plan features without requiring a long-term commitment. The application officially supports Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Mozilla Firefox, while Safari is supported with limited functionality. Customers wishing to update their personal information can do so by visiting the Account Settings page, modifying the required details, and saving their changes.

Customers who want to cancel their subscription can submit a cancellation request through the Billing section of the customer portal. Since subscription cancellations affect customer accounts and billing, these requests are reviewed according to company policy before confirmation. If the application crashes during normal operation, customers should clear their browser cache, verify uploaded file sizes, update their browser, and retry the operation before contacting Technical Support. Customers requesting permanent account closure must contact the Account Support team, complete identity verification, and obtain approval before the account can be permanently deactivated.

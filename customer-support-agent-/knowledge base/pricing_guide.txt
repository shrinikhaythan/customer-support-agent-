ABC Technologies offers a cloud-based business management platform that enables organizations to manage projects, customer relationships, invoicing, workflow automation, reporting, and team collaboration through a secure web-based application. The platform is available in multiple subscription plans to meet the needs of startups, growing businesses, and enterprises.
ABC Technologies offers multiple subscription plans to accommodate businesses of different sizes and operational requirements. The Starter
Plan is priced at $19 per user per month and is designed primarily for startups and small businesses. It includes user management features, basic reporting capabilities, email support, and five gigabytes of secure cloud storage. Customers looking for an affordable solution with essential business management capabilities generally choose this plan.
The Professional Plan costs $49 per user per month and includes all features available in the Starter Plan along with advanced analytics, workflow automation, API access, priority email support, and one hundred gigabytes of cloud storage. This plan is suitable for growing businesses that require additional automation and integration capabilities.
The Business Plan is available at $99 per user per month and includes every feature offered in the Professional Plan, together with multi-department collaboration, role-based access control, audit logs, dedicated phone support, and one terabyte of cloud storage. This subscription is intended for medium-sized organizations that require enhanced collaboration, security, and administrative control.
For large organizations, ABC Technologies provides an Enterprise Plan with custom pricing based on business requirements. Enterprise customers receive unlimited users, a dedicated account manager, single sign-on integration, enterprise-grade security, unlimited cloud storage, custom software integrations, and premium technical support available twenty-four hours a day throughout the year. Organizations interested in the Enterprise Plan should contact the Sales department to receive a customized quotation.
All subscription plans include a fourteen-day free trial for new customers. Customers choosing annual billing instead of monthly billing receive a fifteen percent discount on the total subscription cost.
